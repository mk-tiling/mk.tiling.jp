About

This site is a personal archive and toolset maintained by MK, dedicated to the exploration of mathematical tilings and geometric patterns through both theoretical foundations and algorithmic experimentation.

A notable result is the Modulo Krinkle tiling, a family of non-periodic tilings, describable using elementary tools and exhibiting a distinct form of structural regularity.

For more details, see the paper on arXiv:2506.07638.

You can also find more of my work in the Mathematical Tiling and Tessellation Facebook group.
